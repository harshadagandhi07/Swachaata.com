What we do?

Services we provide

1. Residential Waste Collection

  • Regular curbside collection of household waste.
  • Collection of recyclables such as paper, plastic, glass, and metals.
  • Special pickups for bulky items like furniture and appliances.
  • Yard waste collection including leaves, branches, and grass clippings.

2. Industrial Waste Management

  • Handling and disposal of industrial waste generated from manufacturing and production processes.
  • Management of hazardous waste materials, including chemicals, solvents, and byproducts.
  • Provision of waste containers and on-site waste management solutions.

3. Recycling Services

  • Sorting and processing of recyclable materials.
  • Operation of recycling facilities and drop-off centers.
  • Educational programs and initiatives to promote recycling among residents and businesses.

4. E-Waste Management

  • Collection and recycling of electronic waste such as computers, televisions, and mobile phones.
  • destruction services to ensure the secure disposal of electronic devices.
  • Refurbishment and resale of usable electronic components.

5. Education and Outreach

  • Education programs to promote waste reduction, recycling, and sustainability.
  • Workshops and seminars for businesses and schools on effective waste management practices.
  • Development of educational materials and resources.
